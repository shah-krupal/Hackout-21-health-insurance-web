About Us

On the 5th anniversary of our involvement in the healthcare sector, it is our pleasure to transition into the insurance and health model. 2021 marks the establishment of DHK insurance, registered under MSMED (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises Development). Here, we focus on providing customers with end-to-end custom health and insurance services and solutions by integrating customer needs with our mission to accelerate the spread of sustainable and health oriented culture. Thusfar, DHK has been limited to over 10 major cities. In the near future, we will be extending our service area to all regions of Gujarat. With regards to customer base, we have experience with 10K customers and more. Companies, such as high-rise residential complexes, AAA bank and APP hospitals are major corporate client served In order to deliver to this growing demand, we are scaling up our existing 25-member engineering and 10-member policy developer team, who have over 10 years of collective, relevant, and diverse experience in the industry.
